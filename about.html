I am currently the Senior Graphic Designer at the University of Canterbury, where I lead a creative team of designers.

I have been working in the graphic design industry for over 20 years and graduated from Wanganui School of Design (University of Waikato), where I received a Bachelor of Computer Graphic Design Degree (BCGD) with distinction for academic achievement. I also studied at Yoobee (Natcoll), where I received a Diploma in Computer Graphic Design (dip.CGD).

I have a wide range of skills using Adobe Creative Suite: InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Acrobat Professional, After Effects, Express, Firefly (AI), as well as Microsoft Office, Visual Studio Code, and Google Web Designer. I’ve also used project management tools including Monday.com and Streamtime.

Throughout my career, I’ve worked with several design firms including Q Brand Agency, Waves Advertising/Crescendo, and MLT, and I’ve provided freelance services to various companies.

At the University of Canterbury, my responsibilities include creative design, typography, and layout for both print and digital media. I provide leadership and management, overseeing creative direction and branded projects. My role also involves scheduling, art direction (including photography), and client communication with both internal and external stakeholders. I’m an effective problem solver and consistently meet strict deadlines while collaborating closely with Account Managers and other team members.

In support, Caroline Polson, TwoScoops Co-founder, Client Services and Managing Director (previously UC Client Services and Account Manager), is willing to act as a referee on my behalf.
